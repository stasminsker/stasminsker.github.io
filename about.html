Bio

I received B.Sc. in Mathematics from the Novosibirsk State University in 2007 and Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2012. Prior to joining USC in Fall 2015, I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at Duke University (2012-2014) and worked in Quantitative Analytics at Wells Fargo Securities (2014-2015).
